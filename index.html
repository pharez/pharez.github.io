Pharez Aouad is a photographer and Filmmaker based in Accra, Ghana. His work is predominantly influenced by his life and observation of the experiences of others around him. His passion and ability to read light makes his still and motion work cinematic; a gripping piece of artistry regardless of the client or project.

He currently resides in Accra but travels where ever his work takes him.
